# Customer Onboarding (White-Label Basic)

This onboarding is for sales and delivery communication only. No source code transfer is implied.

## 1) Discovery and scope lock

- Confirm target audience and deployment model.
- Select Model A or Model B from `COMMERCIAL.md`.
- Freeze agreed feature bundle at capability level.

## 2) Identity and platform setup

- Collect brand kit (name, logo, colors, store texts).
- Collect buyer domains and hosting preferences.
- Confirm Apple/Google account ownership model.

## 3) Infrastructure readiness

- Provision server and database according to checklist.
- Configure DNS and TLS.
- Prepare operational accounts and least-privilege access.

## 4) Content and launch preparation

- Prepare initial content sets (news/blog/announcements).
- Validate notification test-device workflow.
- Run sanitized screenshot review for public materials.

## 5) Go-live and support handoff

- Execute go-live checklist.
- Share support channels and incident flow.
- Confirm support cadence and monthly operations model.

## 6) Security guardrails during onboarding

- Demo credentials are shared privately and rotated as needed.
- No credentials are ever stored in public repositories.
- Public artifacts remain capability-level only (no internals).

## Turkce

Bu onboarding akisi satis ve teslimat iletisimine yoneliktir. Kaynak kod devri anlamina gelmez.

## 1) Kesif ve kapsam kilidi

- Hedef kitle ve deployment modelini netlestir.
- `COMMERCIAL.md` icinden Model A veya Model B secimini yap.
- Anlasilan ozellik paketini capability seviyesinde dondur.

## 2) Kimlik ve platform hazirligi

- Marka kitini topla (isim, logo, renkler, store metinleri).
- Alici domain ve hosting tercihlerini netlestir.
- Apple/Google hesap sahipligi modelini kararlastir.

## 3) Altyapi hazirligi

- Sunucu ve veritabani hazirligini checklist'e gore tamamla.
- DNS ve TLS konfigurasyonunu yap.
- Operasyon hesaplarini least-privilege prensibiyle hazirla.

## 4) Icerik ve canliya cikis hazirligi

- Ilk icerik setlerini hazirla (haber/blog/duyuru).
- Bildirim test-device akis dogrulamasini yap.
- Public materyaller icin sanitize screenshot kontrolunu tamamla.

## 5) Go-live ve destek devri

- Go-live checklist adimlarini uygula.
- Destek kanallarini ve incident akislarini paylas.
- Destek periyodu ve aylik operasyon modelini netlestir.

## 6) Onboarding sirasinda guvenlik sinirlari

- Demo erisim bilgileri ozel kanal ile paylasilir ve periyodik dondurulur.
- Public reposlarda credential saklanmaz.
- Public artefactlar capability seviyesinde kalir (ic implementasyon yok).
